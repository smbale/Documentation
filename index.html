Welcome to FinTech Lusaka Hackathon.

Theme

This Hackathon brings together FinTech developers, ideators and designers to develop financial products & services and apply new technologies to solving problems. Example applications include innovations in risk management, trading, investing, personal finance, big data, retail banking, regulatory compliance, mobile payments and social enterprise.
